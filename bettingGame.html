Betting Game

In this game you get to guess a number between 1 and 10 and place a bet between $5 and $10. If you guess correctly, you will win the amount you bet. If you guess incorrectly you will lose the amount you bet. If you guess a number that if off by 1 your don't win or lose the bet (you get nothing and you lose nothing). You start with $100 in the bank and you can play until you run out of cash.
